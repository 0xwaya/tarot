# Design Brief for QueenCity Soundboard Latin Acoustic Series Campaign

## Objective
Create a cohesive and compelling visual identity that highlights the Latin Acoustic Series and ties it to QueenCity Soundboard and the new Cincinnati event promoter webapp.

## Audience
- Bilingual or culturally connected Cincinnati audiences looking for intimate live music experiences.
- Younger professionals and creatives who respond to elevated design more than nightlife clichés.
- Venue partners and artists who need promotion materials that feel credible and premium.

## Deliverables
- Logo refresh to include Latin acoustic elements
- Social media templates (Instagram posts, Stories, Facebook banners, TikTok backgrounds)
- Event posters and digital flyers
- Color palette focusing on warm, earthy tones mixed with modern accents
- Typography guidelines for a professional yet vibrant feel
- A reusable launch key art system for future acoustic-series drops

## Style & Tone
- Blend cultural authenticity with contemporary design
- Vibrant, inviting, and music-oriented
- Emphasize warmth, community, and artistic passion

## Visual Direction
- Warm neutrals, dark espresso tones, aged paper textures, and selective gold or copper accents.
- Photography and layouts should feel intimate and close-cropped, not festival-wide or generic nightlife.
- Favor typography with character and restraint over loud club aesthetics.

## Timeline
2 weeks for initial draft and revisions

## Notes
Coordinate closely with content team to ensure messaging consistency and brand cohesion.

Avoid:
- flags, maracas, or stereotyped Latin visual shortcuts
- EDM-style neon palettes
- template poster layouts that look interchangeable with generic event promos
