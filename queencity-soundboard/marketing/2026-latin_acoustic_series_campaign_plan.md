# QueenCity Soundboard Latin Acoustic Series Marketing Plan

## Campaign Goal
- Fill the first Latin Acoustic Series launch run with a high-intent local audience before paid acquisition becomes necessary.
- Position QueenCity Soundboard as the curator of intimate Latin acoustic nights, not just another event listing surface.
- Build a repeatable campaign structure that can support future artist-night drops with minimal rework.

## Primary Audiences
- Cincinnati and Northern Kentucky Latin music listeners looking for smaller, more intentional live events.
- Bilingual young professionals who want culturally relevant programming without generic nightlife branding.
- Local artists, venues, and community partners who can amplify launch posts and ticket intent.

## Success Metrics
- 150 waitlist signups before the first public ticket push.
- 3 partner reposts from local artists, venues, or community organizations each cycle.
- 5% or better click-through from social content to the event or waitlist page.
- 8% or better conversion from landing-page visits to ticket-click intent.

## Branding & Design Upgrades
- Refresh logo and visual identity to emphasize Latin acoustic vibes + Cincinnati roots.
- Create consistent design assets: cover photos, profile pics, event posters, social media templates.
- Develop a color palette blending warm acoustic tones and modern tech aesthetics for the webapp.
- Build a style guide for all visuals and messaging to ensure brand coherence.

## Messaging Pillars
- Intimate: small-room energy, close access, human performance.
- Cultural: rooted in Latin identity without cliché visual language.
- Local: Cincinnati-centered, community-led, easy to attend.
- Curated: every event should feel selected, not mass-posted.

## Social Media Account Profiles
- Establish/optimize accounts on key platforms: Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, TikTok, YouTube.
- Create a LinkedIn page for B2B event promoter networking and partnerships.
- Use local Cincinnati hashtags and Latin music event tags.
- Cross-link all profiles to each other and the new webapp for lead capture.

## Content Creation Strategy
- Create a content calendar with:
  - Teasers and artist highlights for Latin Acoustic Series.
  - Behind-the-scenes videos and rehearsal snippets.
  - Educational posts on Latin acoustic music culture.
  - Webapp feature spotlights and user guides.
  - User-generated content campaigns with event attendees.
- Engage local influencers and Latin community leaders for content collaborations.
- Plan live streams or short acoustic sets to build excitement.

## Launch Sequence
- T-21 days: announce the series identity and waitlist CTA.
- T-14 days: introduce the first featured artist and venue mood.
- T-10 days: publish a short rehearsal or acoustic teaser.
- T-7 days: open the main ticket push with a single conversion CTA.
- T-3 days: post logistics, arrival vibe, and what to expect.
- Event day: publish reminder, live-story coverage, and post-show capture prompts.

## Multi-Agent CEO Recommendations & Integrations
- Automate daily social media posting schedules via multi-agent bot integrations.
- Use AI content generators to draft captions, tweets, and blog blurbs.
- Integrate analytics agents to track engagement, follower growth, and campaign ROI.
- Set up customer support chatbots for webapp inquiries and lead nurturing.

## Final Phases to Ship & Launch
- Phase 1: Branding refresh and social media account creation.
- Phase 2: Launch teaser campaign and introductory webapp soft launch.
- Phase 3: Content ramp-up with artist spotlights, community engagement.
- Phase 4: Official Latin Acoustic Series announcement with live events.
- Phase 5: Webapp event booking and promotion full launch.
- Phase 6: Post-launch analysis, feedback gathering, and iterative content adjustment.

## Additional Tooling or Integrations Recommended
- Social media scheduling: Buffer, Hootsuite, or open-source alternatives.
- AI content tools: OpenAI GPT, Jasper, or integrated custom agents.
- Analytics: Google Analytics, Facebook Insights, and custom LangGraph agents.
- CRM for lead capture and email marketing integration.
- Streamlined event registration via webapp with calendar sync.

## Operator Notes
- Each post should carry one CTA only: join waitlist, get tickets, save the date, or share.
- Avoid generic festival copy; keep the voice closer to private-show energy.
- Use bilingual phrasing selectively, but keep the core CTA immediately legible.
